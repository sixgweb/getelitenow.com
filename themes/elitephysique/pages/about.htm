About Us

Elite Physique

Located in the heart of Rio Rancho, New Mexico, Elite Physique Gym is a premier fitness facility dedicated to helping you reach your goals — whether you're just starting out or training for competition.

{# ── OUR STORY ──────────────────────────────────────── #}
Our Story

Built for Athletes

Elite Physique Gym was founded with a simple mission: create a space where people of all fitness levels feel welcome, supported, and empowered to become the best version of themselves.

Our ACE-certified trainers bring years of experience across personal training, group fitness, strength & conditioning, and nutrition coaching. Whether you're looking to lose weight, build muscle, or improve overall wellness, we have the expertise and equipment to help you succeed.

From state-of-the-art equipment to a welcoming community atmosphere, everything at Elite Physique is designed to challenge the athlete in you.
